Active Recovery - Light stretching or yoga


Cardio

Recovery is crucial for maximizing gains and preventing burnout. Ensure you get enough quality sleep, maintain a balanced diet, and stay hydrated throughout the day. Incorporate active recovery activities like light stretching or walking on rest days to promote circulation and muscle repair. Listen to your body's cues, adjust your training intensity accordingly, and remember that progress happens not just during workouts, but also during recovery. By prioritizing rest and recovery, you'll optimize your performance and stay on track towards your fitness goals.
